Explore the Rivers

Discover the beauty of nature through breathtaking river adventures. Below are some of the most iconic rafting destinations, each offering a unique experience.


Main Fork of the Salmon River, Idaho

Nestled deep in the Frank Church Wilderness of No Return, the Salmon River is a haven of natural beauty, lined with towering pine trees and teeming with wildlife. Experience Class I-IV rapids and sandy beaches perfect for family adventures.

Desolation and Gray Canyon, Utah

Travel through Utah's most rugged canyons on the Green River. With stunning desert landscapes and Class II/III rapids, this 84-mile trip is perfect for history enthusiasts and thrill-seekers alike.

Grand Canyon, Arizona

The ultimate river adventure! Navigate world-class whitewater while marveling at the grandeur of the canyon. From exhilarating rapids to ancient ruins, this is an experience of a lifetime.
